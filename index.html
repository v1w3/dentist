about Us

Our exciting world of dentisty is constantly advancing. I am priviledged to have studied my BDS Dentistry degree at the University of Witwatersrand. I am constantly upgrading my skills and learning new techniques through numerous courses. I have furthered my studies at WITS, UNISA, The Implant and Aesthetic Academy, UP and other institutions.

I am a member of the SA Dental Association,Health Professions Council (HPCSA),Alpha Omega International and a number of special interest groups.

I have over 20 years experience and regularly attend courses to upgrade my skills. Our study groups for advancement in dentistry include dental specialists from the various disciplines.

  • Root treatment & tooth abscesses
  • Gum treatment(periodontics)
  • Minor orthodontics & childrens dentistry

Initial consultation

Your first consultation will include a detailed examination and a few X- Rays in order to assess your dental health. At the consultation you will be given the opportunity to meet our dental team and to discuss your concerns and treatment options.

The following aspects will be assessed:

  • Teeth, crowns,roots, restorations
  • Tooth to tooth relationships the bite and skeletal jaw relationships
  • Supporting tissues, gums, periondontal tissues and salivary glands
  • Calculus, tartar & plaque
  • Grinding, bruxing,muscles, muscle spasms, and joints
  • Aesthetics
